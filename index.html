    What are the key benefits of Turmlitegold Capsules?

    Promotes Weight Loss- This weight loss supplement assists in healthy weight reduction by helping burn fat effectively and efficiently.

    Increases Metabolism- Naturally increases the body's metabolic rate and can be consumed in combination with Nutrixgold Juice or Diagemax Juice for improved outcomes.

    Neutralizes Excess Sugar- Neutralizing excess sugar in the body can help lower LDL (bad) cholesterol levels, promoting better metabolic health.

    What is Turmlitegold Capsule, and how does it help in healthy weight loss? Does it have any other benefits?

    Preserva Wellness Turmlitegold Capsule is a weight loss supplement that contains five components, namely Garcinia, Green Tea, Garlic, Methi, and Curcumin (Turmeric extract). The plant-based capsule helps metabolize the fat and lipids in the body effectively and boosts the metabolic rate to aid in healthy weight loss. It reduces the synthesis of fatty acids in the liver and adipose tissue, promotes the burning of fat and sugar, and neutralizes excess lipids in the body. In addition to weight loss, the capsules also have the benefit of reducing the risk of diseases associated with obesity.

    What is the dosage for Turmlitegold Capsules?

    For optimal results, it is advised to consume two capsules in the morning after breakfast on a daily basis.

    How do the ingredients of Turmlitegold Capsules help in weight management?

    Curcumin (Turmeric extract), Garcinia Indica, Methi, Garlic Powder & Green Tea aid in healthy weight loss. Garcinia Indica contains hydroxycitric acid that inhibits fat and cholesterol synthesis and controls blood sugar and hunger pangs. Fenugreek induces fullness and promotes weight loss. Garlic neutralises free radicals, improves energy levels, and manages weight. Green tea boosts metabolism. Curcumin is a natural fat-burner and anti-inflammatory, ensuring healthy and long-lasting weight loss without drastic results. Turmlitegold capsules offer a slow, effective, and long-lasting weight loss solution, preventing the weight from returning once usage is stopped.

    What is the shelf life?

    The recommended shelf life of 100% vegetarian Turmlitegold Capsule is 36 months from the date of manufacture. It is advisable to store the supplement in a cool and dry place away from direct sunlight.

    Turmlitegold Capsules are ideal for whom?

    • Turmlitegold capsules aid in healthy weight loss and are suitable for individuals with various health conditions and lifestyles.
    • Turmlitegold Capsules are effective in managing weight in individuals with diabetes, hypothyroidism, and PCOD. The hormonal and metabolic imbalances associated with these diseases make it difficult to lose weight, but regular consumption of Turmlitegold Capsules can help.
    • Chronic alcohol consumption is known to cause weight gain and increase the risk of fatty liver disease and other health conditions. Turmlitegold Capsules can aid in weight management in such cases.
    • A sedentary lifestyle with a lack of physical activity and sitting jobs can lead to weight gain, making it difficult to lose weight. Turmlitegold Capsules can help in weight management in such situations.
    • Lack of sleep has been linked to weight gain and obesity. Turmlitegold Capsules can assist in controlling weight gain associated with sleep loss.
    • Stress is a major contributor to weight gain, and Turmlitegold Capsules can help manage weight in individuals with high levels of stress.
    • Deficiency of vitamins and fiber in the diet can cause the body to store fat and energy. Turmlitegold Capsules are rich in essential nutrients that can improve overall nutrition and aid in weight management.
    • For best results, it is recommended to take Turmlitegold capsules in combination with Nutrixgold Juice or Diagemax Juice.

    Are the capsules effective for thyroid disorders?

    Thyroid problems occur due to hormonal imbalances and changes in metabolism. Turmlitegold Capsules help with weight loss, which occurs during hypothyroidism and can be effective for people suffering from the disease. It is best combined with Thyropro Juice for people with Hypothyroidism.
